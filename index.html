The idea

Safety information lives everywhere — and nowhere.

A pothole on your street is talked about in a WhatsApp group. A power outage is reported on Twitter with the wrong location. A theft happens down the road and neighbors only hear about it days later.

There is no shared, real-time picture of what's safe and what isn't. Safe IQ is being built to be that picture — owned by the community, not by any platform.

What's inside

Four pillars.

Report in 30 seconds

Pick a category, drop a pin, snap up to 5 photos, add a quick note. Done. Your report is live for everyone in the area.

Alerts, only for near you

A live map of what's happening around you. Critical hazards trigger push notifications for your specific area. No spam from across the city.

One-tap SOS

Emergency? Send a beacon to the nearby community with your location, in one press. No fumbling through menus. When it matters most, it's already ready.

Verified by the community

Every report has a confidence score built from neighbor verifications. Real problems get attention. Fake ones get filtered out.

Under the hood

Every choice earned.

Safe IQ is a solo project. Every part of the stack was chosen deliberately, and the whole thing runs on free tiers so the community pays nothing.

Client

Jetpack Compose — 100% modern Kotlin UI

Compose BOM 2024.05, Kotlin 1.9.24, AGP 8.7.2. Material 3 theming, edge-to-edge, dark mode by default. Hilt for DI, Room for local caching where it counts.

Maps & location

Google Maps SDK + FusedLocationProvider

maps-compose 4.4 with marker clustering. Dynamic map padding tracks the bottom-sheet position so the location dot always stays visible. Auto-recenter on user bearing after gesture idle.

Backend

Firebase Spark plan — free tier, forever

Auth with email/password + Google. Firestore for reports, users, verifications, comments. Cloud Messaging for geohash-topic push. Security rules with email-allowlist admin check.

Media

ImgBB for hazard photos

Firebase Storage requires the paid Blaze plan — we don't pay. ImgBB is a free public image host. JPEG compression to 1600px, EXIF rotation, up to 5 photos per report.

Intelligence

Google Gemini for the AI Assistant

Direct REST API, no Cloud Functions needed. Streaming responses with retry-on-failure. Location + report context injected into the prompt. Voice input via speech recognition, voice output via TTS.

Privacy

You own your data. Full stop.

No analytics SDK. No ads SDK. No third-party tracking anywhere. Location is used only for map centering and nearby alerts, and never leaves your device unless you post a report at that location.
