The problem

Safety data lives everywhere — and nowhere.

A pothole on your street is talked about in a WhatsApp group. A power outage is on Twitter with the wrong location. A theft happens down the road and neighbors only hear about it days later.

There's no shared, real-time picture of what's safe and what isn't. Safe IQ is being built to be that picture — owned by the community, not by any platform.

What's inside

A safety toolkit for real neighborhoods.

Six pillars that work together to make everyday safety information usable, verifiable, and shared.

Report in 30 seconds

Pick a category, drop a pin, snap up to 5 photos, add a quick note. Done. Your report is live for everyone in the area.

Alerts, only for near you

A live map of what's happening around you. Critical hazards trigger push notifications for your specific area — no spam from across the city.

One-tap SOS

Emergency? Send a beacon to the nearby community with your location, in one press. No fumbling through menus. When it matters most, it's ready.

Verified by community

Every report has a confidence score built from neighbor verifications. Real problems get attention. Fake ones get filtered out.

My parking

Save where you parked. Multiple spots supported. Directions back to your car open in Google Maps. "Which floor was it?" — solved.

AI safety assistant

Ask questions about safety in your area, or get a plain-language summary of what's happening nearby. Powered by Gemini with your location context.

How it works

Three moves. Everyone safer.

1

You see something

A pothole, a leaking transformer, an unsafe spot at night. Open Safe IQ, tap Report.

2

The area gets alerted

Everyone within your neighborhood sees the report on their live map. Critical hazards trigger push notifications.

3

The community verifies

Neighbors confirm or reject. Confidence builds. Real problems get attention. Fake ones filtered.

Under the hood

Every choice earned.

Safe IQ is a solo project. Every part of the stack was chosen deliberately, and the whole thing runs on free tiers so the community pays nothing.

Client

Jetpack Compose — 100% modern Kotlin UI

Compose BOM 2024.05, Kotlin 1.9.24, AGP 8.7.2. Material 3 theming, edge-to-edge, dark mode by default. Hilt for DI, Room for local caching where it counts.

Kotlin 1.9.24 Compose BOM 2024.05 Hilt

Maps & location

Google Maps SDK + FusedLocationProvider

maps-compose 4.4 with marker clustering. Dynamic map padding tracks the bottom-sheet position so the location dot always stays visible. Auto-recenter after gesture idle.

maps-compose 4.4 Places SDK 3.5

Backend

Firebase Spark plan — free tier, forever

Auth with email/password + Google. Firestore for reports, users, verifications, comments. Cloud Messaging for geohash-topic push. Security rules with email-allowlist admin check.

Firestore Auth FCM

Media

ImgBB for hazard photos

Firebase Storage requires the paid Blaze plan — we don't pay. ImgBB is a free public image host. JPEG compression to 1600px, EXIF rotation, up to 5 photos per report.

ImgBB API EXIF

Intelligence

Google Gemini for the AI Assistant

Direct REST API, no Cloud Functions needed. Streaming responses with retry-on-failure. Location + report context injected in prompt. Voice via SR + TTS + NLP.

Gemini REST Voice I/O

Privacy

You own your data. Full stop.

No analytics SDK. No ads SDK. No third-party tracking anywhere. Location used only for map centering and nearby alerts — never leaves your device unless YOU post a report.

Zero tracking On-device
